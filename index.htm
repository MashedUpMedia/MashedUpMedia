About Us

Based in Midlothian our goal is to bring new and original web design to our local area, at respectable prices. Started in 2011 MashedUpMedia is a brand new company started by two close friends, Paul Macfarlane and Calum Graham. With a combination of photography, graphic design and computing backgrounds the team at MashedUpMedia is a strong one. We are always looking to expand our own knowledge and experience to provide high quality services and ultimate customer satisfaction.

Our Services

We offer companies the opportunity to expand over the ever changing technological market. We understand it can be difficult and expensive to keep up with technologies, but don't worry we can do it for you! MashedUpMedia offers standards compliant website design and branding as well as Apple and Android app development. Whether it is one or all of these sectors you wish for your business to expand into, we have a package for you.
