A little bit about who we are...

Based in Midlothian our goal is to bring new and original web design to our local area, at respectable prices. Started in 2011 MashedUpMedia is a brand new company started by two close friends, Paul Macfarlane and Calum Graham. With a combination of photography, graphic design and computing backgrounds the team at MashedUpMedia is a strong one. We are always looking to expand our own knowledge and experience to provide high quality services and ultimate customer satisfaction.

Calum Graham

About:
For the past 4 years I have been working as a freelance photographer. Along side this I have also been working in some of the biggest clubs and bars in Edinburgh, such as Shanghai, Le Monde, Vodka Revolution, The Three Sisters, Club Hive and Fankensteins. This has given me a vast amount of venue experience and has helped greatly in developing my skill set. With the nature of my working experience, I have gained professional skills in photo editing and photo manipulation inside programs such as Photoshop CS5. This along with educational and industry knowledge in graphic design I am a highly confident professional designer.
